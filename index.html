FAQ

What all services are provided under computer repair services?

Computer repair services involve the repair and maintenance of multiple devices ranging from desktops and laptops to micro components of these devices. Some of their main services include fixing hardware and software issues, retrieving lost data, OS installation, etc.

How do I know when I need the help of a Star Computer?

There are some issues which can be quickly sorted by yourself. For example, a minor virus can be fixed by installing an anti-virus. However, if any of your problems persist, it is advisable to reach out to a professional who is certified to work with these gadgets.If the servicing is not done on time, the life of the device/system might depreciate considerably.

What steps should I take before giving my computer for repair?

Before giving your computer for repair, it is very important to follow the below steps:

  1. Create a backup of all the data
  2. Put essentials on the cloud so that you can access it even from other systems
  3. Erase confidential information like bank account details, tax details, etc. from your computer
  4. Provide the system passwords to the repair professionals so that they can freely work on the system
